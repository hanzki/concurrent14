The file dubinski.tab contains a simple (free format) table of 7 columns and 
81920 rows: the masses and six phase space coordinates (x,y,z,vx,vy,vz) of 
81920 particles that define the initial conditions for a MW/Andromeda 
collision. Contact John Dubinski (dubinski@cita.utoronto.ca) for more details
(see also: Dubinski, Mihos & Hernquist, 1996, ApJ, 462, 576). Its the model B
collision discussed in this paper).

The data here may be slightly different than the ones in other similar archives
(e.g. GC3). The 81920 particles are in the following order:

    16384 Gal. disk
    16384 And. disk
    8192  Gal. bulge
    8192  And. bulge
    16384 Gal. halo
    16384 And. halo
